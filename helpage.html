Welcome to Habit-Linked Expense Tracker

Build strong habits while keeping your fun budget in check. Follow this simple 3-step guide to get started.

Step 1: Set Up Habits

Go to the Habit Setup page and create your daily habits. Add name, frequency, and penalty amount for skips.

Step 2: Daily Check-In

Mark your habits as Done or Skipped every day. Skipping deducts money from your Fun Budget.

Step 3: Track Progress

Use the Dashboard to view your streaks and see how your Fun Budget vs Penalty Jar changes over time.
